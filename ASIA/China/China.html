China

Capital: Beijing (Peking)

Population: (2021 est.) 1,416,687,000

Language: Mandarin

Currency: Renminbi

China, officially the People's Republic of China, is a country in East Asia. It is the world's most populous country, with a population of more than 1.4 billion. China spans five geographical time zones and borders 14 different countries, the second most of any country in the world after Russia.

With a long and rich history, China probably has the greatest number of places of historical interest. Only in Beijing can one find countless famous places, including tile Great Wall, the Summer Palace and the Temple of Heaven. A tour to such places is very impressive and one can learn a lot about Chinese history and traditional Chinese culture from it. Moreover, as one of the biggest countries in the world it also has many beautiful scenic spots, such as Guilin and lijiang. These beautiful natural scenery give tourists most wonderful enjoyment and best memories.
